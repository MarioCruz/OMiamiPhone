Kids

🍌 Ericka

The Banana


that is shaped like a phone


that is so bright, it’ll shine the night


that smells like the beautiful sunflower


it has dots, as if it had freckles


it has skin covering the delicious fruit


it is fresh and ripe


the banana is rolling through the city,
looking for a stomach


but it still remains a ripe and lonely banana

🍌 Mikayla Jones

Gibson

(Yellow)
A banana peel open on the floor
an egg yolk in a white bowl
a star bright on a dark night
a sunflower in a freshly planted garden
a hibiscus as bright as the sun
a dandelion like a Laffy Taffy
a freshly squeezed lemon
as bright as a pencil
as bright as my friend's attitude
a sunny day and the banana colored flowers
the color yellow is everywhere
all around you
here and there

Kids

🍌 Vanessa Gil

Emerson Elementary

I love bananas
so much.

I eat it with pancakes.
It is yellow.

It is so squishy
and in the inside
it is white
and in the outside
it is yellow.

It is
like the
clouds.

It smells
bad.

It tastes like
heaven.
